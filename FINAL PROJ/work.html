Magazine

Graphic Design 2 - Spring Semester 2023 8.5” x 11” Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign

The goal of this project was to create a magazine on the topic of our choice. This offered us the freedom to create an issue that we were passionate about. In our magazine, we had to include one large article, two short articles, an infographic, icons, experimental type, and images. In a modernistic and vintage style, I chose to highlight my favorite band, Fleetwood Mac. I wanted the viewer to feel as though they could pick this up in either 1973 or 2023. As a result, I highlighted the integrity and styles that would have been prevalent in both periods.

Sequential Diagram

Graphic Design 3 - Fall Semester 2023 Illustrator

Matryoshka Dolls, or 'Russian nesting dolls,' are iconic in Russian culture and commonly found in stores, including some in the United States. Beyond being toys, they are prized collectibles and souvenirs, meticulously hand-painted for a sophisticated touch. Crafting involves intricate planning, precise woodwork with linden trees, shaping with wood turners, adherence to strict painting guidelines, varnishing, and a quality check before reaching store shelves.
